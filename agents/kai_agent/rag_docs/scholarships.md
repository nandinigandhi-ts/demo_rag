# Scholarships

We offer need-based and merit-based scholarships.

## Need-based
- Requires income proof and a short statement of need.
- Typical award: 10% to 40% off the tuition fee.

## Merit-based
- Based on prior academics or portfolio (if applicable).
- Typical award: 10% to 30% off the tuition fee.

Scholarship decisions are communicated within 5 business days.
